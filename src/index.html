About Us

Serving our neighbors with dignity and compassion.

Our Mission

The Laingsburg Area Food Bank is dedicated to fighting hunger in our community. We believe that no one should go without food, and we work to ensure that families and individuals have access to nutritious meals when they need them most.

Our Story

Since 1985, we have served the Laingsburg area through the generosity of local volunteers, churches, businesses, and community members. What started as a small effort to help neighbors in need has grown into a vital community resource, distributing thousands of pounds of food each year.

Who We Serve

We serve residents of the Laingsburg School District, including Laingsburg, Woodhull Township, Victor Township, and surrounding areas. Anyone in need is welcome—no questions asked.

What We Offer

Free food assistance for our neighbors in need.

Food Distribution

Fresh produce, canned goods, dairy, bread, and pantry staples. Take what you need to feed your family.

Emergency Assistance

Facing an unexpected crisis? We provide immediate food assistance outside regular hours when possible.

USDA Commodities

We distribute USDA commodity foods through the Emergency Food Assistance Program (TEFAP).

Resource Referrals

Need additional help? We connect families with other community resources and assistance programs.

Who We Serve

No Requirements

We believe in serving with dignity. You do not need to provide:

  • Proof of income
  • Identification
  • Proof of address
  • Referral from an agency

What to Expect

Your first visit is simple:

  • Come during our open hours
  • Tell us your household size
  • Select food based on your family's needs
  • Visit as often as you need
