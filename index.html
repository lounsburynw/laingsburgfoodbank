Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use the food bank?

Anyone in the Laingsburg School District area who needs food assistance. This includes Laingsburg, Woodhull Township, Victor Township, and surrounding areas.

What do I need to bring?

Nothing! We don't require ID, proof of income, or proof of address. Just come during our open hours.

How often can I visit?

As often as you need. There are no limits on how frequently you can receive food assistance.

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment needed. Just walk in during our regular hours: Monday 1-3 PM or Thursday 5-7 PM.

What food is available?

We offer fresh produce, canned goods, bread, dairy, proteins, and pantry staples. Availability varies, but we always have a good selection.

Is the food free?

Yes, completely free. We're here to help our neighbors, no strings attached.

Can I donate food?

Absolutely! Drop off non-perishable items during our regular hours. Most needed: canned proteins, peanut butter, cereal, and canned vegetables.

How can I volunteer?

Contact us at (517) 651-5531 or laingsburgumc@gmail.com. We need help with sorting, stocking, and distribution.

Are monetary donations accepted?

Yes! Financial contributions help us purchase fresh food and cover operational costs. Contact us to arrange a donation.

Is the food bank a registered nonprofit?

We operate through Laingsburg United Methodist Church. Contact us for information about tax-deductible donations.

About Us

Serving our neighbors with dignity and compassion.

Our Mission

The Laingsburg Area Food Bank is dedicated to fighting hunger in our community. We believe that no one should go without food, and we work to ensure that families and individuals have access to nutritious meals when they need them most.

Our Story

Since 1985, we have served the Laingsburg area through the generosity of local volunteers, churches, businesses, and community members. What started as a small effort to help neighbors in need has grown into a vital community resource, distributing thousands of pounds of food each year.

Who We Serve

We serve residents of the Laingsburg School District, including Laingsburg, Woodhull Township, Victor Township, and surrounding areas. Anyone in need is welcome—no questions asked.
